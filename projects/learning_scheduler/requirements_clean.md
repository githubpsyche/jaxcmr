## E. Under‑appreciated details reviewers will look for (and many applicants miss)

* **Demo‑ability.** The MVP must **run live** at demo day with compelling **metrics** (not just screenshots).
* **User & partner validation.** Mention **named user communities** (students/instructors) and **how feedback will be collected**.
* **Data/ethics/privacy.** Not spelled out here, but any education tool implies **ethics approval** and **data handling**; stating this in one line signals professionalism.
* **How ML‑engineering support is used.** Don’t just say “we’ll use it”; specify **what chunk of work** you’ll hand to them (e.g., API hardening, scaling, telemetry).
* **Sustainability & hand‑off.** A sentence on **post‑sprint maintenance/ownership** and **IP/open‑source stance** helps the “path to partners” objective.
* **Registration for the pitch.** The application asks you to **confirm availability**; forgetting this is an easy own‑goal.
